Folder with scripts for versions 1.0, 1.1, and 1.2.

Names with "primrec" mean primitive recursion is used.
Names with "ppr" mean parameterized primitive recursion is used.
All those will not work with 1.0 or 1.1, only 1.2 or beyond.
Likewise disordered scripts like add3reord will only work with 1.2.
